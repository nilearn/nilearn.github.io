4.4. nilearn.image: Image processing and resampling utilities¶

Mathematical operations working on niimgs like -a (3+n)-D block of data, and an affine.

Functions:

high_variance_confounds(niimgs[, ...]) Return confounds signals extracted from input signals with highest variance.
smooth_img(niimgs, fwhm) Smooth images by applying a Gaussian filter.
resample_img(niimg[, target_affine, ...]) Resample a Nifti image
reorder_img(niimg[, resample]) Returns an image with the affine diagonal (by permuting axes).
crop_img(niimg[, rtol, copy]) Crops niimg as much as possible
mean_img(niimgs[, target_affine, ...]) Compute the mean of the images (in the time dimension of 4th dimension)
swap_img_hemispheres(niimg) Performs swapping of hemispheres in the indicated nifti.
